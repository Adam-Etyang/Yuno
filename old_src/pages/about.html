Our Story

Born from the frustration of endless email chains, double bookings, and missed opportunities, Yuno emerged as a solution to the chaos that often defines campus resource management.

We witnessed students struggling to reserve auditoriums for their presentations, faculty dealing with scheduling conflicts, and administrators drowning in paperwork. There had to be a better way.

Our team of passionate developers, designers, and campus life enthusiasts came together with one mission: to create a platform that would streamline, simplify, and enhance the entire campus booking experience.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every campus operates like a well-orchestrated symphony, where resources are utilized efficiently, and where students and faculty can focus on what truly matters ‚Äì learning, growing, and creating.

Yuno isn't just a booking platform; it's a catalyst for better campus experiences. We're building technology that connects people, optimizes spaces, and creates opportunities for meaningful interactions.

From small study group reservations to large-scale conferences, we believe every event deserves the perfect space and seamless planning experience.
